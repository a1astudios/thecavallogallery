Giuliano Cavallo

Born in Viña del Mar Chile, Giuliano Cavallo traveled trought out South America and was influence at early age by the Arts of Drawing, Painting and Signing. After winning an Art contest he traveled to Miami Beach Florida in 1987 to continue his studies. in 2001 he published his collection of Art named Abstract Difussion featured in "Madonna in Art" and "Diana in Art by pop art books London UK.

Piya Whilhelmy

Piyanut Wilhelmy-Cavallo was born in the early ‘70s in Bangkok/Thailand and moved to Saarbruecken/Germany with her parents when she was still a little child. Being the only child of her German father Arthur Wilhelmy and her Thai mother Mrs. Somjintana Wilhelmy, her relationship to her parents was very intense and harmonious. Her work covers Oil, Acrylics, Watercolours and Sculptures.
